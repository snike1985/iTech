Infrastructure Discover our work environment and our services

Our 10.000m² facility is located at the center of the fast-growing Charleroi Brussels South Biopark where startups can benefit for the full investment incentive package of the Walloon Region like R&D grants, reduced labor tax and reduced patent income revenue tax.

Incubator

Our incubator is a mixed building with fully equipped offices and lab modules of 15m² to 150m² with direct access to parking. It benefits from the international Biopark ecosystem which makes talent recruitment easier for startups. Facilities expansion is also facilitated in the Biopark thanks to the industrial allocation of the surrounding land.

Meeting Rooms

We have 5 meeting rooms for up to 80 people fully equipped with telecom and display solutions.

Reception

Our standard office management services include visitors and parcels reception, bilingual phone permanence, copy facilities, mail posting and catering solutions.

We are located next to Charleroi Brussels South Airport providing interesting logistics solutions for your samples.

Our DNA

Our mission is to accelerate the fund-raising process of startups by activating around them the right set of sharp competences. We cover the 8 key elements needed for the development of startups: scientific proof of concept, market validation, business model, intellectual property strategy, funding options, team identification, outsourcing partners and regulatory requirements. Deliverables are professional business plans, investor slide decks, C-level management team, advisory board members, strategic connections and VC introductions.

The way we work with startups is based on acceleration programs which happen partly on an individual basis and partly with short and intense boosters on a cohort basis. Startups can apply at any point in time to our programs. Selection is based on an interview with our CEO and with our program managers who will pitch your application in front of our selection committee. We are proud to consider startups who completed a program with us as part of our active Alumni network.
